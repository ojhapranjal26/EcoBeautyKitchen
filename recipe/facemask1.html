8 Dermatologist-Approved Hydrating Face Mask Recipes

Reviewed by: Dr. Jennifer Haley, M.D., FAAD

Post may contain affiliate links.

No matter your skin type, be it oily, dry, or somewhere in between, everyone can benefit from adding a boost of hydration to their skincare regimen. And while you can find many hydrating face masks at beauty stores, the key ingredients may already be in your fridge or pantry.

Natural ingredients like honey and cucumber for your skin

1. Hydrating Face Mask for Normal Skin

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons organic raw honey (warmed)
  • 1 teaspoon cottage cheese

Honey is antibacterial and full of antioxidants. Cottage cheese adds selenium and vitamin D, perfect for moisturizing and smoothing the skin.

2. Hydrating Face Mask for Combination Skin

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg white
  • 1 avocado

This mask balances oils while hydrating and purifying the complexion with proteins and healthy fats from egg whites and avocado.

Applying your homemade hydrating mask

3. Hydrating Face Mask for Dry Skin

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized carrots
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 2 teaspoons organic raw honey

Carrots provide a calming, nourishing effect, while butter adds essential fats for dry skin. This mask is ideal for giving a gentle sun-kissed glow.

4. Hydrating Face Mask for Oily Skin

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • ¼ cup strawberries

Strawberries, rich in vitamin C, help fight oily skin while yogurt nourishes and exfoliates. Perfect for removing excess oil and lightening acne scars.

5. Hydrating Face Mask for Sensitive Skin

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • ¼ tablespoon organic turmeric powder

This mask brightens and calms sensitive skin with lemon’s antibacterial benefits, milk’s soothing properties, and turmeric’s anti-inflammatory effects.

6. Hydrating Face Mask for Acne-Prone Skin

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoon aloe vera gel
  • 1 tablespoon oatmeal

Oatmeal cleanses and soothes, while aloe vera fights bacteria and moisturizes acne-prone skin.

7. Hydrating Face Mask for Glowing Skin

Ingredients:

  • Aloe vera gel
  • 2 teaspoons vitamin C powder

This mask is rich in antioxidants, helping to brighten and enhance the skin’s natural glow by boosting collagen production.

8. Hydrating Face Mask for Mature Skin

Ingredients:

  • Avocado
  • 2 drops vitamin E oil
  • 1 teaspoon organic turmeric powder

This mask, packed with healthy fats, vitamin E, and turmeric, helps protect aging skin from damage and supports its overall health and hydration.
