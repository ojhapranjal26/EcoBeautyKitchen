Our Mission

We believe in the power of nature. Our mission is to guide you through creating your own eco-friendly skincare with organic ingredients.Join us and discover the beauty of homemade skincare.



Beauty Recipe Index

You don't need harsh chemicals to be a natural beauty. Just steal these gentle, back-to-basics recipes + DIYs to bring out your natural glow.


  • Skin Care

    Revolutionize your skincare routine by skipping toxic ingredients entirely! Yes, it's true, you can opt for DIY creams, moisturizers and masks, all of which are natural and actually good for you.

  • Cleansers

    Homemade cleansers are not just great for removing your makeup, but also for getting rid of dirt and toxins that may have accumulated during the day.

  • Toners

    Rosewater? Witch hazel? Apple cider vinegar? Which one makes the best homemade toner and what other options are there? Find the answers to all these questions and more in this section of EcoBeautyKitchen.

  • Lips Care

    Pamper and nourish your lips with our collection of DIY lip care recipes.

  • Face Masks

    We've got a natural face mask for everything: acne, blackheads, dry skin, enlarged pores, aging skin, you name it! Whatever the problem, it can probably be remedied the natural way: with food on your face!

  • Lotion, Body Butter + Body Oil

    Moisturize and nurture your skin with homemade body lotions, body butters and body oils. It doesn't get any more luxurious than that!

  • Body Scrub

    Learn how to make your own DIY body scrubs - whether it's with sugar, salt, or coffee.
