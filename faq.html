Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is EcoBeautyKitchen?

EcoBeautyKitchen is a collection of homemade skincare recipes using natural ingredients to promote sustainable beauty.

2. Are these recipes safe for all skin types?

Our recipes use natural ingredients, but we recommend conducting a patch test before full application to avoid allergic reactions.

3. How long do these homemade products last?

Most recipes last for about 1-2 weeks when stored in a cool, dry place. Since no preservatives are used, the shelf life may be shorter.

4. Where can I find the ingredients?

You can find the ingredients for our recipes at local grocery stores, health food stores, or online retailers.

5. Can I modify the recipes?

Yes, feel free to adjust the recipes based on your skin type or preferences, but be cautious of how certain ingredients may interact.
